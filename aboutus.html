James D. Moffatt is the Pastor

of Church Unlimited,

based in Indio, California.

Pastor James' passion is to break down the walls of religion and build a ministry that is diverse, unified and well grounded in the foundational truths of God's word.

Pastor James is a licensed minister through the Pentecostal Church of God. He holds his degree in Theology through The Sure Foundation Theological Institute.

He and his wife Cheryl live in the Palm Springs area with their two daughters.

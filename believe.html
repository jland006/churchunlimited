We believe...

That the Word of God as contained in the Holy Bible is


I. inherrant in its' content

II. absolute in its' authority

III. complete in its' revelation


Psalm 68:11 | II Timothy 3:16-17 | II Peter 1:20-21


In One God, existing in three persons, (Trinity or Tri-unity)


I. God the Father

II God the Word/Son, Jesus

III. God the Holy Ghost

Genesis 1:1-3 | John 15:26 | II Corinthians 13:14 | I John 5:7


In the Deity and Lordship of Jesus Christ, that He is


I. God in the flesh

II. The Lamb that takes away the sins of the world

III. Lord Over the Church


Isaiah 7:14 | Matthew 1:23 | John 1:29 | Colossians 1:18


In the Salvation of mankind through confessed faith

I. confess with your mouth the Lord Jesus

II. believe in your heart that God has resurrected Him

III. live according to the confession of your faith


Romans 10:9-10 | I Corinthians 15:1-4 | I Timothy 4:12-16


In the Baptism of the Holy Ghost, and that it is


I. subsequent to salvation

II. a necessity for every believer

III. essential for kingdom building

IV. accompanied initially by the evidence of unknown (other) tongues

Acts 1:8, 2:4, 10:46, 19:2 | Romans 8:9 | I Corinthians 14:2


That Water Baptism is a rite of the church, that is an outward expression of an inward experience, and that it


I. shows that the believer is dead to sin

II. shows that the old man had been buried with Christ

III. shows that he has been resurrected in the newness of life


Romans 6:3-4 | I Peter 3:21 | Acts 2:38


In Holy Communion and Feet Washing, that these too are rites of the church, and they show

I. that you are one with Christ

II. that you believe in and walk according to His teachings

III. that you partake in this rite, to memorize His death


John 13:8, 14-15 | I Corinthians 11:27-28 | Luke 22:19


The Church Fellowship is


I. essential for spiritual growth

II. a command of God to corporately worship together

III. a place that is ordained of God, to increase in knowledge and strength

I Peter 2:1-2 | Hebrews 10:25 | Acts 20:27-28


That the Great Commission of the church is to


I. go into all the world and preach the gospel to every creature

II. make disciples of believers

III. establish the kingdom of God on earth


Matthew 28:18-20 | Acts 1:8 | John 4:1 | Revelation 11:15

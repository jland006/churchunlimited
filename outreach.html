Gas Giveaway - On December 15th Church Unlimited gave away $7,000 in free gas to the people in the Coachella Valley. In two hours, 280 vehicles were blessed with $25 worth of gas! No strings attached...the 281st car driven by a woman who prayed that we'd be able to help her out with anything was blessed with a full tank of gas! That's how our God works, He's an UNLIMITED GOD! A God that does above and beyond anything we could ask. Church Unlimited is here to touch lives, make a positive impact on the Coachella Valley and win people for Christ!

Jump Start - we adopted the children from Shelter from the Storm and our local Sheltering Palms Foster Family Agency and gave backpacks filled with school supplies and a new pair of shoes to 65 children. We've also reached out to a school for orphans in Kenya, Africa called New Dawn Communities.

Food Drive for the Find Food Bank - we donated 1559 pounds of food which is equivalent to over 11,000 meals.
